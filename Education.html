Education Background

I graduated from high school in 2019 with a solid academic record and active membership in the National Honor Society. Following graduation, I enrolled at Metropolitan Community College (MCC) and began taking pre-nursing courses. However, after researching various topics, I discovered a passion for computer science. In 2022, I decided to change my major to computer science at MCC. This transition was motivated by my deep interest in technology and problem solving. To further my education and career prospects in computer science, I transferred to the University of Missouri-Kansas City (UMKC).
